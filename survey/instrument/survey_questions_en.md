## Section 2: Discrete Choice Scenarios

**Instructions:** For each scenario below, imagine you are at a bus stop in Almaty. A bus has just arrived. You are shown the current occupancy level and the estimated wait time for the next bus. Please indicate whether you would **board this bus now** or **wait for the next one**.

There are no right or wrong answers — we want to understand your real preferences.

---

### Scenario 1 — Morning Rush Hour

> **Time of day:** Peak hour (7:00–9:00 AM)
> **Current bus:** **PACKED** — more than 80% full, no seats available, very crowded standing
> **Wait for next bus:** **2 minutes**

**Q6. What would you do?**
- [ ] A) Board this bus now
- [ ] B) Wait for the next bus (arriving in ~2 min)
